# Landing Page Architect

## Overview

This skill provides a proven 8-step architecture for creating high-converting landing pages. It guides the creation of landing pages that effectively capture leads and sales by following a specific psychological flow.

## 8-Step Landing Page Architecture

Follow this specific sequence to simple, yet effective landing pages.

### 1. HERO
**Job**: Get email or get scroll

Structure:
- **Eyebrow**: Short, punchy context setter
- **Headline**: The main value proposition
- **Subheadline**: Supporting detail or explanation
- **CTA**: Clear Call to Action
- **Trust Signals**: Logos, ratings, or brief social proof to build immediate credibility

### 2. SUCCESS (Conditional)
**Job**: Kill buyer's remorse

*Note: This section is conditional, often used after a sign-up or purchase.*

Structure:
- **Checkmark**: Visual confirmation of success
- **Confirmation**: "You're in!", "Order received", etc.
- **Deliverable list**: Exactly what they are getting now

### 3. PROBLEM-AGITATE
**Job**: Make status quo painful

Structure:
- **3 Problems with Agitation**: Identify 3 specific pain points and twist the knife to show why they act as barriers.
- **Personal Transition**: Bridge the gap between the problem and the solution (the product/service).

### 4. VALUE STACK
**Job**: Make saying no feel stupid

Structure:
- **4 Tiers Descending**: List value items from most to least valuable.
- **Total Value**: tangible dollar amount or immense relative value.
- **Your Price**: The offer price, which should seem small in comparison to the total value.

### 5. SOCIAL PROOF
**Job**: Let others convince them

Structure:
- **Header**: "Don't just take our word for it"
- **3 Testimonials**: specific results-oriented quotes from real users.

### 6. TRANSFORMATION
**Job**: Make outcome tangible

Structure:
- **4 Stages**:
    1.  **Quick Win**: Immediate benefit.
    2.  **Compound**: Medium-term benefit.
    3.  **Advantage**: Long-term competitive edge.
    4.  **10x**: The ultimate, life-changing result.

### 7. SECONDARY CTA
**Job**: Catch the scrollers

Structure:
- **Avatar Stack**: Visuals of users (e.g., face pile).
- **Question Headline**: Re-engage with a question.
- **"Yes" Button**: A low-friction agreement or action.

### 8. FOOTER
**Job**: Professional legitimacy

Structure:
- **Logo**: Brand identity.
- **Nav**: Essential links only (don't distract).
- **Legal**: Terms, Privacy, etc.
- **Social**: Social media links.
